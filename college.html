About GEC Kishanganj

Government Engineering College, Kishanganj (GEC Kishanganj), established in 2019, is a premier institute under the Department of Science, Technology & Technical Education, Government of Bihar. The college is affiliated with Bihar Engineering University and approved by AICTE. The lush campus is located by the scenic Mahananda River.

Departments

  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Computer Science (AI & ML)
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering

  5. Wi-Fi Enabled Campus

Quick Facts
